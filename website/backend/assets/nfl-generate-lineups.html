Help Menu

Use these tools to select the perfect lineup. Click on "Mass Shuffle" and the system will shuffle all players who are not "HOLD", or select the "Shuffle" button beside each player and shuffle that player and position.

If you want to create multiple lineups/cards, simple select the "Multiple Card" button and the system will generate up to 22 cards. Once you build your lineup, click save, and compare your picks with the winning player and build up your player models.

Advanced Options

Selecting "$100" and "$2000" salary range, will show lineups within the max salary range ($58,000 - $59,900)
