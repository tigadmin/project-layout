Help Menu

The stats table shows a side by side comparison for all players that are playing in your daily fantasy contest. Select the "Position" button to see player stats by position.

Why are "Player Indicators"?

If you see a player indicator (ex. "!"), simply hover over the player, to see important notes about the player, such as player injuries.

Player Alerts Color Key:

    Red = "Not Playing" - Player is not playing
    Green = "Probable" - Player should play, but not 100%
    Yellow = "Questionable" - Player may or may not play

How it Works?

This gives you an overview of all players playing in your contest. See below for some quick tips:

    Use the "Display" drop down to show specific players.
    Select the "Focus" tab to show a highlighted view of stat rows for a specific person.
    Select the "Position" button to show the different player positions
    Look for "!" status indicators to see important player updates.

What are Player Trends?

Player trends shows a graph of each stat category based on last 30 days of data. This allows you to see if a player is HOT or Not based on exact stats from last 30 days.

What are Player Models?

Player models are used by our software in order to pick the best possible picks. By default we show our internal "Player Model". For best results create your own "Player Model", this allows you to create your custom player models, by assigning a numerical value to each stat, that you deem important.

To create your own player model:

    1. Click the "Player Model" dropdown and choose "Create New" from dropdown
    2. Assign a numerical value between (1-1000) to each stat category

On stat table, then select your "Player Model", this will recalculate the top players based on your "Player Model"

What is "AV value"?

The AV value is a nummerical value, that each player has generated based on your "Player Model". The higher the AV value, the better the selection according to the "Player Model".

What is "View Player Rank"?

The player rank generates the top 15% of all players based on your "Player Model". The player rank table show the top 15% of players for each player position based on your player model.
